About Dogs

The dog (Canis familiaris or Canis lupus) is a domesticated descendant of the wolf.
The dog has been selectively bred over millennia for various behaviors, sensory capabilities, and physical attributes.

Dog facts

  • Dogs can identify smells as dilute as one part per trillion (That's one drop in twenty Olympic-sized swimming pools!)
  • We can see 180 degrees; your average dog can see 240 degrees, depending on breed
  • Dogs can hear frequencies of 45kHz compared to our 20kHz max
